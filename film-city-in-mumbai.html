Film City In Mumbai: The City Made Of Film Sets And Cameras

Mumbai, the city of dreams, has a heartbeat that pulses through the veins of the Indian film industry. One of its most iconic landmarks is Film City, a magical place where hundreds of films, TV shows, and advertisements are shot yearly. Known for its elaborate sets and buzzing with cameras and action, Film City is a must-visit for movie buffs and anyone curious about the behind-the-scenes of Indian cinema.

About Film City

Located in the lush greenery of Goregaon, Film City, officially named Dadasaheb Phalke Chitranagari in honor of the father of Indian cinema, is a grand spectacle. Spread over 520 acres, this sprawling complex houses 20 indoor studios and massive outdoor sets that replicate bustling city streets, villages, and even grand palaces. Film City is a mini-world of grandeur, providing the perfect canvas for filmmakers to bring their stories to life.

Film City was established in 1977 by the Maharashtra government and is now one of the most significant production hubs in the country. From Bollywood blockbusters to soap operas and reality TV shows, the wide range of content shot here cements its status as the nucleus of India's entertainment industry.

Touring Film City In Mumbai

Ever wondered what it's like to walk through the sets of your favorite movies? Film City offers you that exclusive opportunity. Visitors can explore this star-studded world through guided tours, a privilege that allows you to delve into the heart of Indian cinema. These tours take you through various studios and outdoor sets, and if you're lucky, you might even catch a live shoot in progress.

During the tour, you'll walk through iconic film sets, learning about the techniques and efforts that go into creating cinematic magic. The guides provide fascinating insights about the movies shot here, from construction details to the secrets of special effects.
