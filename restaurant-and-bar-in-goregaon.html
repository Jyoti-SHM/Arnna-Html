Nadara Restaurant & Bar in Goregaon

Savour a culinary journey of west-coastal India & beyond at Nadara

Nadara meaning nature, a newly opened multi-cuisine restaurant, is poised to enhance dining in the vibrant Goregaon neighborhood of Mumbai. Inspired by the flavours of the west coastal belt of India, the menu features dishes from Maharashtra, Konkan, Mangalore, and Kerala. The offering is further complemented by tantalising kebabs influenced by the kitchens of Turkey, Afghanistan, and Bangladesh along with Italian specials on the menu.

If you’re looking for a delightful dining experience, our beautiful restaurant in Goregaon offers a warm and welcoming atmosphere for guests of all ages. We take pride in serving a diverse menu that caters to all tastes, making it the perfect choice for family gatherings. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply enjoying a meal together, our attentive staff is here to ensure a memorable experience at our family restaurant in Goregaon.

Your Go-To Restaurant Near NESCO Goregaon

Conveniently located near NESCO Goregaon, our restaurant is ideal for both locals and visitors. With ample parking and a cozy ambiance, it’s the perfect spot to unwind after a long day or to gather with friends. Our chefs use the freshest ingredients to create mouthwatering dishes that will satisfy your cravings. Don't miss out on the opportunity to experience the best dining in Goregaon at our restaurant, where great food and great company come together.

Nadara distinguishes itself with a commitment to quality ingredients and traditional cooking techniques, steering clear of fusion in an attempt to relive the lost regional flavors. Founder Akshat Shetty emphasizes, "Nadara, is a celebration of regional food treasures, all crafted with time-honored traditional techniques such as slow cooking, Dum, BBQ, and more. The presentation, keeping pace with tradition and modernity, is contemporary”. Nadara takes pride in its eco-friendly approach, using service ware crafted from natural stones, pebbles, terracotta earthenware, and coconut shells, promoting a connection with nature.

Discover Great Flavors at the Best Restaurant in Goregaon East

Nestled within Arnna, 3 star hotel in Goregaon , Nadara aims to be a top dining destination for both international guests and local residents seeking an exceptional culinary experience. We warmly welcome you to our restaurant in Goregaon, conveniently located near NESCO.
